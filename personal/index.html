Researchs

● Hierarchical Expert Recommendation

Under co-supervision of Dr. Roy Ka-Wei Lee and Prof. Julita Vassileva • Sep 2019 – Present

Utilizing attention-based hierarchical neural networks to model the user’s expertise and questions in a community question answering platform (Stack Overflow in this study) in order to retrieve experts who can answer a given question.

● Automatic Playlist Generator

final thesis of Bachelor’s degree under supervision of Prof. M.A. Fazli • Jul 2018 – Jun 2019

In this project, we aim to implement playlist generation method discussed in WSDM paper and evaluate effects of using topic modeling and sentiment analysis of lyrics for playlist generation.

● Sentiment Analysis of Digikala reviews

under supervision of Prof. H. Beigy • Jul 2018 – Jun 2019

Digikala is an online Persian market. In this project, first we try to analyze sentiment of user ratings in Persian language using neural network. After sentiment analysis of reviews we will rate products based on these sentiments.
